Myths & Miss-Information


Most of us have a strong desire to make positive changes in our lives. Eat healthier, lose weight, stop smoking, be a better parent, a better partner, be more productive, start a new business. However, there is often a big gap to bridge between what we want and what we do. The attempts to make the changes we want often fail, the results are disappointing. We tell ourselves that change is too hard; we lack willpower or motivation. None of this is true; the problem is in the approach. If you have tried to make changes in your life and they have come up short, please don't assume it's your fault; it isn't. Society would have us believe otherwise; of course, sadly, the world we live in is one of blame and criticism.

For years, myths, misconceptions, and poor advice with no scientific grounding set us up to failâ€”each failure providing ammunition to our limiting beliefs. Getting past blaming ourselves , past our limiting beliefs, is essential to our success.

Mind First provides you with a step-by-step process to get what you want, and it is much easier than you think. You will gain confidence as you move towards the change you wish to. Importantly, you will learn to feel good about yourself as you recognize and celebrate the small wins you make every day on the path of transformation. Right now, this may seem a long way off, but this method works; thousands have followed the same path to success, and you can too.
